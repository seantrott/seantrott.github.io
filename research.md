My primary research uses Large Language Models (LLMs) as "model organisms" to test questions about human language and cognition. In turn, I use methods and theories from Cognitive Science to provide insights into how LLMs work: a discipline I call ["LLM-ology"](https://seantrott.substack.com/p/so-you-want-to-be-an-llm-ologist). For more details, see my [lab page](https://seantrott.github.io/lcc-lab/).
